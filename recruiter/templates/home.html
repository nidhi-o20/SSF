About Us

Saraswati Seva Foundation (SSF) is a fast-growing non-profit organization with a vision to reduce poverty and encourage social justice by educating the children, enabling the youth, empowering the women and supporting the farmers. SSF partners with other development agencies and institutions, and promotes grass-root change leadership in the remote regions. Our effort is to work towards building technology-driven and scalable solutions for specific social needs that are environmentally sustainable and commercially viable.

Child

SSF’s programs for children ensure that children in neglected regions and under resourced schools have access to best breed educational content and gain critical life skills alongside 21st century knowledge to prepare them for an ever changing world SSF aims to bridge educational and digital divides through technology adoption.

Youth

SSF assists in developing fundamental skills that are complementary to emerging technologies such as literacy, basic academic skills – together with entrepreneurial, financial, digital, cognitive skills (STEM), understanding that the future lies in workers creating economic value with machines rather than against them.At SSF, youth are trained and leveraged as the ambassadors of change.

Women

A positive change in women’s social and economic status generates a multiplier effect across the value chain.As digital automation can affect men and women differently, women being seriously underrepresented in the digital sector. SSF espouses to reduce gender divide in STEM, IT and computing.At SSF, we work towards making women skilled, connected to contemporary networks to build livelihood and supported with required know-how.
